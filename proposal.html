App Description

Users of Application

Here are the users of the application

  • This web application will be accessible for the general public specifically travelers who value safety or travelling solo for featured destinations.
  • Travelers who prefer traveling to low crime rate locations due to safty concerns.

Information Accessible for the Users

  • The users will be viewing ideas of potential safe destinations.
  • Safest destinations
  • Users can access emergency numbers around the world
  • Emergency numbers
  • Price of each of the dream destination with the sample itineris will be provided.
  • List of the options will be offered to add all the places in users bucket list for travelling.

Expected Interactions For the Users

  • Users can conveniently add their favorite destinations to the itinerary after evaluating potential fees, ticket costs of the tourist attractions they want to visit.
  • Lists of emergency phone numbers used in countries around the world will be provided

Our Goal is to Solve the Travelers Problems

  • List of the Ideal and safe travel destinations with all the safety information and tourist attraction costs will be provided for the users to build their own itinerary on our website.
  • Travelling on a budget will be just a click away!
